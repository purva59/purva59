Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is a historic arch in Mumbai, built in 1924, overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple is a Baháʼí House of Worship in Delhi, known for its lotus-like shape and peaceful ambiance.

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a 73-meter tall minaret in Delhi, India, built in the early 13th century by Qutb-ud-din Aibak and later completed by his successors. It is made of red sandstone and marble and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
